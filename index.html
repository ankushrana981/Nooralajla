Car Rental Required Documents
Driving Licence

To rent a Sport or a Sedan Car in Dubai, you need a Driving Licence or Emirates Id. If you are a tourist coming to Dubai and you want to rent a sports car, you need to provide an international driving licence or passport. We also accept a local driving licence from certain countries. And the most important thing, the driving licence must be valid. Nooralajla Car Rental – Sports Car Hire Dubai is on-demand. Just visit us and get your dream car.

01.
Emirates ID or Passport (For Tourists)

To rent a Sport or a Sedan car in Dubai, you need a Driving Licence or Emirates ID. But as a tourist, you must have an international driving license or passport if you wish to rent a car in an international city. Don’t hesitate to contact us, we will guide you throughout the process. Being in a different country is always a big hassle and we are here to make your journey hassle-free.

02.
Credit Card For The Security Deposit

Hiring a car requires you to leave a security deposit of 1000 AED by a credit card, that will be held for 29 days and will automatically be released. This deposit is essential in case there are any fines on the car, that in most cases appear after 20 days. Your money is absolutely safe with us and it will be given back to you, once the essential time period is over.

03.
Minimum Age

Every individual country has its own rules and regulations, just like this in Dubai to rent a sport or the Sedan car your minimum age should be 21 for normal cars and 23 for higher-end cars. That’s a law and we cannot make any exceptions.

04.
Instructions
  • 1. Salik charges
    In addition to the standard fees, please note that Salik charges will be applied as extra charges. Salik is Dubai's electronic toll collection system, and any toll incurred during the service will be added to the final bill
  • 2. Fuel Policy
    As a standard, our rental cars must be returned with a full tank of fuel. If the vehicle is not returned full, refueling charges plus a service fee of AED 50.00 will apply. We also offer an Advance Fuel Purchase option for your convenience. This allows you to pre-pay for a full tank of fuel, so you can return the car at any fuel level without worrying about refueling or incurring extra charges. Note that there are no refunds for unused fuel.
  • 3. Off Roading Rules
    Off-road driving is not allowed. The customer is fully liable for any damage or additional costs resulting from off-road use. Please adhere to this policy to avoid penalties.
